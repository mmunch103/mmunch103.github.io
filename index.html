About

I was born and raised in Palo Alto, CA and made the move out to the ATL to attend Georgia Institute of Technology. I enjoy mixing my background in Computer Science with my love for design to create unique solutions. I believe being well-rounded and a "Jill of all trades" is what sets me apart from others.

All my skills are self-taught, and I hope to continue to build my repitoire from typography, to animation, to front-end dev. Some other fun facts about me: I have a 200lb dog, I lived in Australia for a year, I also enjoy poetry, volleyball and backpacking, and I have traveled to 20 different countries.

TopTime Coffee Co.

As a Co-Founder and the CMO of this Coffee Technology Startup, I am in charge of the entire brand identity and strategy for our company. The main pieces of this include our website, instagram content, marketing material, and merch. I have also designed the entire customer experience involved with coming to our pop-up shop and buying a cup of coffee off our cart, which I designed and built (shown below).

Although our website is powered by Squarespace, in order to utilize the e-commerce capabilities, I designed the parts and pieces of our site in order to sell our company and tell the story of who we are.

For our Instagram, I have curated a plan that separates us from the generic coffee instagram of latte art and nice lighting. We try to be as creative as possible. Mondays are "Lonely Mug Mondays", where we put quirky adoption stories of our thrifted mugs. On Wednesdays, we post grind art to celebrate the "Wednesday Grind". A variety of gifs and videos are also in our toolkit.
